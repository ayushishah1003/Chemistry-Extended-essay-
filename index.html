Connect to Google Sheets

This checklist saves student progress to your Google Sheet. You only need to do this once.

  1. Open your Google Sheet
  2. Click Extensions → Apps Script
  3. Delete all existing code and paste the contents of Code.gs
  4. Press Ctrl+S to save
  5. Click Deploy → New deployment
      Type: Web app  |  Execute as: Me  |  Access: Anyone
  6. Click Deploy, authorise, then copy the URL
  7. Paste it below and click Save & Test
